Development of an Online Educational Tool on Military Trauma and PTSD

Detail

Leading a team of two graduate students, I developed an online educational tool to raise public awareness about military trauma and PTSD. The tool aimed to educate the public about the unique challenges faced by veterans, providing accessible information on the nature of PTSD and practical ways to support veterans in their communities.
