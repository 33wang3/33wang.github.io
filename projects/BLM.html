Research on Black Lives Matter Movement (BLM)

Research Assistant for Dr. Rezarta Bilali

Conducted research under Dr. Rezarta Bilali to understand the Black Lives Matter (BLM) movement through a case study methodology. Findings were included in the article “Putting the Past into Action: How Historical Narratives Shape Collective Action Participation,” published in the European Journal of Social Psychology in 2021.

Development of Web-Crawl Script

For Racial Justice Data Collection

Created a web-crawling script to gather online messages related to Black racial justice history. This data collection helped capture the discourse surrounding racial justice and identify significant themes in online messaging.

Thematic Analysis

On Racial Justice Historical Narratives

Conducted a thematic analysis to categorize collected data, identifying four main themes within the historical narrative of racial justice. This analysis aimed to understand how different aspects of these narratives influence engagement in collective action and public perception of the BLM movement.
