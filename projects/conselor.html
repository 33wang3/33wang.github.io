Crisis Counselor at New York State Office of Mental Health

Me at Work (Left)

Conducted Counseling Sessions with Clients from Marginalized Groups

Conducted over 100 counseling sessions with clients from marginalized communities, including elderly residents in nursing homes and middle school students. The sessions focused on emotional recovery and resilience-building, addressing the unique emotional challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic and providing a safe environment for clients to process their experiences.

Observation and Identification of Social and Emotional Challenges in Middle School Students Post-Pandemic

Observed two main challenges in middle school students following a year of quarantine and online learning:

  • A lack of social intelligence, which hindered their ability to interact meaningfully with peers.
  • Delayed development in self-expression, evident in their struggles to communicate effectively and connect with classmates.

Design and Facilitation of Group Games and Self-Disclosure Sessions for Students

Designed interactive group games and guided sessions focused on self-disclosure to support students’ social development. These activities helped students practice sharing personal experiences, learn how to express empathy, and build a foundation for healthy social skills and stronger peer relationships.

Media Literacy Workshop for Instructors Experiencing Pandemic-Related Stress

Developed and led a media literacy workshop to alleviate instructors' stress from online teaching and help them understand middle school students’ digital habits. The workshop:

  • Explored the effects of social media on adolescent behavior.
  • Provided strategies to encourage responsible media use.
  • Offered digital self-care techniques to manage screen time and reduce personal digital stress.

Origami and Mental Health Workshops for Elderly Clients

Designed and conducted an origami workshop for elderly residents in nursing homes. This mindfulness activity helped clients engage in focused, creative work, promoting relaxation and mental engagement.

Mental Health Workshop on COVID-19 Impacts

Created and delivered a mental health workshop focused on the emotional impacts of COVID-19. The workshop provided coping strategies and social support techniques and was delivered ten times across different groups to reach a broad audience within the elderly community.
