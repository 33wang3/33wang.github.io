App Design for Boston Children's Hospital

About This Project

Boston Children Hospital offers a platform for ALS patients and clinics to identify the resource options to cope with disease.

The goal is to take clinical decision-making and turn it into a tool that allows ALS patients to look at options and discuss them with their clinician

To see it in action, check the Figma prototype.
